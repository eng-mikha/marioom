About

Marioom is a leading physiotherapy practice in Awlad Saqr, Al Sharkia. Our team of expert physiotherapists specialize in manual therapy, physiotherapy, and sports physiotherapy. We offer a variety of services, including cranio-mandibular dysfunction, pain therapy, training therapy, manual lymphatic drainage, and private patients. Our commitment to sustainable health and wellness is evident in our dedication to providing excellent care and addressing the needs of our patients.

The clinic is under the leadership of Dr. Maryam Al-Saeed.
